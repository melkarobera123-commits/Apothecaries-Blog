The Story of Aspirin

In 1897, chemist :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} working at :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} modified salicylic acid to create acetylsalicylic acid. This compound was safer for the stomach and became the medicine we now call aspirin.
